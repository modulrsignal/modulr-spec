# Non-Goals — Modulr Supply Descriptor Contract

This document defines what Modulr does not do.
It is the most important document in this repository.

---

## Modulr does not bid.

## Modulr does not negotiate.

## Modulr does not price.

## Modulr does not execute media buys.

## Modulr does not operate as a seller agent.

## Modulr does not replace AdCP.

## Modulr does not replace Prebid.

## Modulr does not replace publisher ad servers.

---

## What Modulr does.

Modulr ingests publisher content and emits supply descriptors.
Downstream systems decide how to use them.

---

## Why this document exists.

Neutral infrastructure requires explicit boundaries.
ads.txt, sellers.json, and IRIS.TV are trusted precisely because
their scope is defined and limited.

Modulr's neutrality is not a positioning choice.
It is a structural constraint enforced at the architecture level.

Any feature requiring participation in negotiation, pricing,
campaign management, deal execution, bidding, pacing, or
transaction ownership is out of scope — permanently.
